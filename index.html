Bio

My name is George Kormaris and I am a Greek-born, 23-year-old web developer specializing in the front end of applications. In 2010 I entered the top CS school (highest entrance exam score requirement), in Aristotle University of Thessaloniki.

In 2009, I became interested in Bitcoin and its implications to global trade and economy. I began trading full- time, and by 2012 I quit my studies to pursue trading as a career.

In 2010, during my first semester in college, I also developed an interest in web development. I started by making websites for friends and later freelancing. This slowly turned into my primary passion, and in 2013 I ceased trading in order to take on more projects and start my own company. I then moved to London where I live with my sister.
